Background

I stumbled upon the idea of creating wikipedia-like websites for video games, while studying HTML & CSS. To help with this, I thought about practicing my front-end development skills on creating informational websites about the video games I play. To make this even better, anybody will be able to download these websites for offline use, personal use, and so on.
